Market Opportunity

Europe's battery storage market is entering a rapid-growth phase

Wind and solar will supply more than 70% of new EU generation by 2030 — and storage is the only scalable fix to intermittency. Grid operators across Europe are actively paying record premiums for battery flexibility through FCR, aFRR and capacity markets.

  • RES capacity growing from ~500 GW today to ~900 GW by 2030, driving a structural need for balancing infrastructure
  • Annual BESS investment needed reaches €15bn+ per year — the market is nowhere near supplied
  • European installed BESS capacity growing at 35%+ CAGR through the decade
  • Medium-scale 10–50 MW projects permit faster, achieve better grid access rates and face far less competition than utility-scale

Why Medium-Scale

10–50 MW is the most attractive segment — and our home turf

Medium-scale projects systematically outperform on the metrics that matter: permitting speed, grid connection rates, revenue per MW, and risk diversification — while still delivering the portfolio scale investors need.

  • ⚡

    Faster permitting, higher success rates

    Smaller projects navigate regulatory processes 2–3× faster than assets over 50 MW, with materially higher grid connection approval rates.

  • 🎯

    Better grid positioning, less congestion

    Access to a wider range of grid nodes avoids the revenue compression that plagues large projects at overcrowded substations.

  • 🧩

    Bundling creates scale without the risk

    Aggregating multiple 10–30 MW projects delivers investor-grade scale while individual project risk stays contained. A single permitting failure doesn't kill the program.

  • 🌍

    Proven across European markets

    Active projects and pipeline in Germany, Poland, the Nordics and beyond — with operational experience managing multiple regulatory regimes simultaneously.
